Discover Tawfike's World Of Hobbies

Biking

Tawfike's passion for biking began to flourish after he had to stop playing soccer due to surgery. Looking for a new way to stay active and adventurous, he turned to biking and discovered a whole new world on two wheels.

Biking

Biking isn't just a hobby for Tawfike, it's his favorite hobby. With every turn of the pedal, he finds joy, freedom, and a sense of adventure. This year, he immersed himself even more in his beloved pastime, discovering new paths and creating memories that ride alongside him wherever he goes.

Beach Swimming

Beach swimming is one of Tawfike's favorite hobbies. He adores the feeling of the waves and the sand under his feet. Whether it's a calm day or a bit wavy, he finds joy and relaxation by the water's edge.

Playing cards

Playing cards with friends is a hobby that Tawfike absolutely loves. It's a chance for him to have fun, relax, and enjoy good company. This simple yet enjoyable activity brings laughter and makes memories that he cherishes.
